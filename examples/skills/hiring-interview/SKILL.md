# Hiring & Technical Interview

> **OPTIONAL SAMPLE — adapt to your org or delete.** This is a worked example of
> a hiring SOP for a small team where one tech lead runs the technical track.
> Numbers, roles, and document references are placeholders — replace them with
> your own. Reference: your team's hiring SOP + your engineering playbook + your
> team charter (`docs/CHARTER.template.md`).

## The tech lead is the sole technical interviewer (Glossary (TH): TL สัมภาษณ์เทคนิคคนเดียว)
- The final hire/reject/salary decision belongs to {{OWNER_ROLE}} (manager /
  decision-maker), **not** the tech lead.
- The tech lead **recommends** with written reasoning. (Glossary (TH): TL ให้คำแนะนำพร้อมเหตุผลเป็นลายลักษณ์อักษร)

## Hiring funnel

### Stage 1 — Resume screening
- Does the candidate's experience match the role?
- Red flag: an AI-generated portfolio the candidate cannot explain → reject.
  (Maps to your charter's "explainable work / no rubber-stamping" principle.)
- Record the outcome in your hiring/CRM tool.

### Stage 2 — Technical assessment
- A take-home or test task designed by the tech lead.
- Score against a rubric, not gut feeling.
- Always store the rubric + the result in your hiring/CRM tool.

### Stage 3 — Technical interview (tech lead interviews)
- Ask against the predefined rubric.
- Have the candidate walk through their solution — can they explain it?
- Record answers and score immediately, no delay.

### Stage 4 — Paid trial week (required before any contract)
- Compensation: [your trial compensation] for the trial week.
- A real task plus mentoring.
- Evaluate: code quality, communication, domain understanding.
- Output: a recommendation with reasoning for {{OWNER_ROLE}} to decide.

> This "paid trial before commitment" stage maps to a charter principle such as
> "no advance commitment without verified fit" — keep it only if your charter
> has an equivalent rule.

## Paperwork required before any access (Glossary (TH): เอกสารก่อนให้ access)
Maps to a charter principle on IP / access protection.
- [ ] NDA signed
- [ ] IP assignment signed
- [ ] If the trial passes → employment contract

## Record every rejection (Glossary (TH): บันทึกทุกการปฏิเสธ)
Maps to the charter principle "decisions are written and auditable." Capture the
real reason in your hiring/CRM tool — not just "not a fit."

## Evaluation record (in your hiring/CRM tool)
- Which stage passed / failed.
- Rubric score per criterion.
- The reasoning behind the decision.
